Our Environment

In our modern world we heavily rely on fossil fuel such as gas, oil, and coal. But do we ever consider the amount of carbon emissions our comfortable lives produce and how much impact this has on the environment? Just because we don't see such consequences instantly, does not mean they are already happening.

What Are CO2 Emissions?

Carbon emissions are a type of greenhouse gas that occurs when carbon dioxide enters the air and gets trapped in the atmosphere due to human activity/process like driving vehicles, industry, agriculture, land use, electricity production, and forestry.

CO2 Emissions And The Environment?

Climate change, primiraly the excess of carbon emissions caused by humans is altering our planet's temperature. This in turn causes severe and extreme weather events such as much more severe droughts, tropical storms, wildfires and more. Climate change will not only negatively affect our climate, but crop production and animal's habitats as well.
